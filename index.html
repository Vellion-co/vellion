About Us

Hey everyone, It's 2020, we need to talk about fashion tech. Fashion Tech gets a bad rap, but it shouldn't. Fashion tech's a treasure. Feels great with what we are doing here at vellion apparel to easily shop garments, book fittings, browse fabrics, and view our extentsive catalog.


Shopping traditionally feels like a chore, rather than joy. Something you fall behind on. Something clear out, not cherish. Rather than delight in it, you, deal with it.


With vellion, we've done just that. it's a redo, a rethink, a simplified, potent reintroduction of online shopping and fashion tech as a whole. A fresh start, the way it should be. I will learn and start working with that when I need to.


Thank you for using our service.
